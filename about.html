My Mission

Professional Journey

Dietitian Michelle is a registered Dietitian in Nigeria with over 15 years of experience in clinical practice and nutrition counseling. As a seasoned professional in diet calculation and planning, she has helped thousands of clients achieve their health goals.

Leadership & Entrepreneurship

Michelle is the co-founder of Citadel Nutrition Consult Ltd, where she leads a team of dedicated nutrition professionals committed to transforming lives through evidence-based nutritional interventions.
