Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the first consultation?

The initial consultation includes a comprehensive health assessment, discussion of your goals, medical history review, and development of a personalized approach to your nutrition needs.

How long are the consultation sessions?

Initial consultations typically last 60-90 minutes. Follow-up sessions are usually 30-45 minutes, depending on your needs and progress.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes! I offer both in-person and virtual consultations via video call to accommodate your schedule and preferences.

What are your consultation fees?

Fees vary based on the service and program length. I'll provide complete pricing information when we schedule your consultation to ensure transparency.

Do you accept insurance?

While I don't directly bill insurance companies, I can provide documentation and receipts that you may submit to your insurance provider for possible reimbursement.

Can you help with specific medical conditions?

Yes, I specialize in diabetes management, weight-related issues, and various health conditions. I work collaboratively with your healthcare team when needed.
